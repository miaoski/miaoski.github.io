Philippe Lin / @miaoski

Bio

Philippe is a senior threat researcher in Trend Micro Forward-Looking Threat Research team. He is experienced in interdisciplinary works is always excited by the challenge of acquiring new domains of knowledge. He is experienced in writing server-side (Linux) applications, DevOps, as well as client-side JavaScript. He uses rational database (MySQL / MariaDB) as well as HBase, Hadoop and Pig when he was a data analyst. He was a BIOS engineer which worked on both assembly and UEFI projects, an IPPBX engineer, and a life-time lover of embedded systems. He is recently mainly attracted by Software Defined Radio. Specialties: C, x86 Assembly, Python, MySQL, Linux, Asterisk, SDR.
